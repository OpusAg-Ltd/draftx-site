How it works

One guided pipeline, four steps

DraftX walks every user through the same proven workflow — new starters can't take a wrong turn.

1

Job Pack

Point DraftX at your SolidWorks assembly. It scans every part, validates parameters and flags missing data before anything is exported.

2

Batch Export

Every drawing exported to PDF and every flat pattern to DXF in one run — named, stamped and filed to your conventions.

3

AI Sort

Drawings are classified by operation — laser, folding, fabrication, bought-out — with a review queue for anything uncertain.

4

Release

QC gates confirm the job is complete, job cards print, and nest-ready DXFs flow to Lantek with NestX.

Every step is tracked, so Analytics shows exactly how many hours and pounds DraftX saves you.

Features

Everything between design and the shop floor

📦

Job Pack validation

Scans the full assembly, checks part parameters per company conventions and produces a part-by-part readiness report before export.

📤

Batch PDF & DXF export

One click exports every drawing and flat pattern in the job — consistently named, stamped with units and filed automatically.

🤖

AI drawing sorting

Classifies drawings by manufacturing operation with your own categories. Uncertain calls go to a visual review queue, never silently wrong.

✎

Built-in DXF editor

CAD-grade snapping, measuring and editing for quick flat-pattern fixes — with hand-edits protected from being overwritten by re-exports.

✅

QC gates

A pipeline checklist proves every job pack ran, every file exported and every drawing sorted — before anything reaches the shop floor.

🔆

NestX Add-on

Prepares nest-ready jobs for Lantek directly from sorted DXFs — material, thickness and quantities included.

📋

Job cards & batch print

Auto-generated job cards and one-click batch printing of full drawing sets, grouped by operation.

📊

Savings analytics

Hours and pounds saved per job, per user and per week — the ROI report your MD wants, generated automatically.

🧭

Guided mode

Tabs unlock as each workflow step completes, with a clear "next step" prompt — new users are productive on day one.
